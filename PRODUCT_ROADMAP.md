# TriPepe Product Roadmap

## Product Vision
The app should help users decide: what to do today, where to do it, how to fuel it, how to adapt it if life gets in the way, and how to connect it to races, routes, and friends.

## Core Principles
1. Reduce friction between plan and execution
2. Make every workout actionable in the real world
3. Support triathletes, runners and endurance athletes who are not pros
4. Prioritize usefulness, clarity and retention over gimmicks
5. Keep UX calm, practical and motivating
6. Build in a modular and scalable way
